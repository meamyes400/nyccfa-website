What We Offer

Everything You Need to Break Into Finance

From exclusive internship postings to hands-on workshops, we provide the tools and connections to launch your career.

Exclusive Internships

Access curated finance internship postings from top banks, hedge funds, and financial firms across NYC, many shared exclusively with our members.

Cross-Campus Network

Connect with driven students from 8 NYC universities. Build relationships that extend beyond your campus and last throughout your career.

Career Resources

Interview guides, resume templates, financial modeling primers, and mentorship from students who've landed roles at top firms.

Speaker Series

Hear from finance professionals, alumni in the industry, and recruiters who share insights on breaking into investment banking, consulting, and more.

Skill Workshops

Hands-on sessions covering Excel modeling, valuation techniques, market analysis, and technical interview preparation with industry practitioners.

Industry Events

Networking mixers, firm visits, and panel discussions that put you face-to-face with professionals and recruiters at NYC's leading financial institutions.
